2. Data Collected via Google OAuth

When you sign in with Google, SulfurAI may access:

  • Basic Google account information (email, name, profile image)
  • YouTube account metadata (read-only)
  • YouTube Analytics data (read-only)

OAuth scopes used:

  • openid
  • email
  • profile
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.readonly
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/yt-analytics.readonly

SulfurAI does not upload, edit, or delete YouTube content.

3. How Data Is Used

  • Authenticate users
  • Display YouTube channel and analytics information
  • Provide features explicitly requested by the user

No personal data is sold or used for advertising.

4. Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored in encrypted form
  • Stored data is protected by reasonable security measures
  • No credentials are exposed publicly

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only while your account remains active or as required to provide the service.

You may request deletion at any time by contacting the operator.

6. Data Sharing

Data is not shared with third parties except:

  • Google APIs, as required for service operation
  • Legal compliance if required by law

7. Your Rights

  • You may revoke OAuth access in your Google Account settings
  • You may request access or deletion of stored data by email

8. Self-Hosted Deployments

If you deploy SulfurAI yourself, you are the data controller for that deployment and must provide your own privacy policy and OAuth credentials.
